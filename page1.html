BACKGROUND

After installing a CCTV system for a family member a few years ago, we discovered that there was a need for cost effective CCTV and IT Sollutions for homes and small businesses in Jamaica. 4K Digital was established in 2017 to provide just that. The 4K Digital team is made up of certified, experienced persons who have worked in the Information Technology field for many years.
