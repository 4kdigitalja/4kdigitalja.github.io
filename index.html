SMART is an acronym for System Monitoring Alert Response Technology.

Our DVRs & NVRs conduct automatic self-diagnosis of the surveillance system and send periodic status reports to you. This will notify you:

1. If there are any system failures, or if the system is not recording.
2. When the DVR or NVR requires service.
