BACKGROUND

After installing a cost-effective CCTV system for a family member a few years ago, we discovered that there was a need for cost effective CCTV systems for homes and small businesses. 4K Digital was established in 2017 to provide reliable, cost-effective CCTV solutions for all Jamaicans. The 4K Digital team is made up of certified, experienced persons who have worked in the Information Technology field for many years. We strongly believe in Jamaica and wish to do our part in making it a safer place for future generations.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to consistently provide high quality affordable CCTV solutions for customers and to be the preferred choice for customers who seek added security for their homes and businesses.

OUR VISION

A safer Jamaica for one and all.
